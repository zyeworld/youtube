## Flag

[Flags of the World(FOTW)](https://www.fotw.info/flags/country.html)

> A website devoted to vexillology. Contains lots of valuable information along with decades-old quips of many enthusiasts.
> Not really an 'academic source' per se, but one of the most vast and reliable sources about flags.
